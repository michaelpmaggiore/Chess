Chess Game Instructions

Welcome to the Chess Game! Follow these instructions to play:

  1. Click on a piece to select it.
  2. Valid moves will be highlighted in the grid.
  3. Click on a highlighted cell to make a move.
  4. Play alternates between white and black.
  5. Capture opponent's pieces by moving to their positions.
  6. Checkmate the opponent's king by capturing it to win!
  7. This is our take on Chess! Play it with your friends!
